The future of cryptocurrencies we were promised
We were promised a world of cryptocurrencies that could be exchanged peer-to- peer over decentralized networks. But what has arisen are centralized gatekeepers that operate more like traditional banks, and blockchains that can’t readily communicate with one another. But that is all about to change. Enter Ferrum: the world’s first peer-to- peer network with built in support to read across all blockchains

HOW IS THIS POSSIBLE?
This sounds too good to be true? By utilizing directed acyclic graph (DAG) ledger technology, plug-ins, and concepts such as Proof of Burn, External Proof, and Futures, Ferrum is able to read across networks and represent almost any digital asset, allowing those digital assets to be instantaneously exchanged and transacted in the Ferrum network with near-zero fees. We have published the technical yellow paper for the technically minded who wants to dive deep.
